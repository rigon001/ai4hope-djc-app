About this Study

You are invited to help us test an educational chatbot that provides information about dementia and palliative care. This chatbot is designed to answer questions and provide support to people living with dementia and their caregivers.

What Will Happen

If you agree to take part, you will:

  • Use the chatbot to ask questions about dementia and palliative care
  • Rate how helpful the answers are
  • Share your thoughts about using the chatbot
  • Answer a few questions about yourself (such as your age and country)

This will take about 1–2 hours of your time.

Your Privacy

We will keep your information private and safe. Your name will not be used in any reports. Only the research team will see your information, and it will be stored securely. The data is protected according to the policies of University of Maribor: https://www.um.si/en/o-univerzi-en/document-center/protection-of-personal-data/

You Can Choose

Taking part in this study is your choice. You can stop at any time, and you do not need to give a reason. This will not affect any care or support you receive.

Introduction

You are invited to participate in a research study that aims to develop and validate an AI-powered, personalized education module for dementia and palliative care. Your input as a family caregiver is essential to ensure the educational content is relevant, supportive, and appropriate for caregivers' needs.

Purpose of the Study

The goal of this study is to create a trustworthy, adaptive educational module for people living with dementia and their caregivers. We seek your input to evaluate and improve the quality of AI-generated responses, ensuring the system meets high standards of accuracy, empathy, and ethical compliance.

What Does Participation Involve?

  • Review and rate AI-generated responses to dementia and palliative care queries for relevance, clarity, empathy, and usefulness
  • Assess the trustworthiness of cited sources
  • Identify any concerns about the information provided
  • Provide feedback on the usability of the educational module
  • Complete a brief demographic questionnaire at the start of your participation

Your participation will involve evaluating approximately 15–20 queries. The estimated time commitment is 1–2 hours.

Confidentiality and Data Protection

Your privacy and the protection of your personal data are of utmost importance. The research team handles all information in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The data is protected according to the internal policies of University of Maribor: https://www.um.si/en/o-univerzi-en/document-center/protection-of-personal-data/

  • Types of Data Collected: We will collect demographic data and your evaluations of AI-generated responses. No unnecessary personal data will be collected.
  • Purpose of Data Use: All data will be used exclusively for research purposes to evaluate and improve the AI-powered education module.
  • Data Processing: Your data will be processed in a secure environment at University of Maribor. Only authorized research team members will have access to it. Data will be anonymized to protect your identity.
  • Data Storage: All digital data will be stored on encrypted servers with strict access controls and will be retained for at most 5 years after the study ends.
  • Data Sharing: Data will not be shared with third parties except as required by law, and only anonymized or aggregated data will be used in scientific publications.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without providing a reason, and this will not affect any care or support you or your family member receives.

Risks and Benefits

There are no anticipated risks from participating in this study. Your contribution will help improve educational resources for dementia caregivers and may benefit other caregivers in the future.
